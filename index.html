about
Hello World,
My name is Max Takano and I have a passion for making games. I am a avid game maker, using my education at the University of California, San Diego to improve my programming skills and game quality. I currently study computer science and specialize in 3d Graphics. On the side I have multiple game projects on my own and with teams that I spend all of my free time on.

I have two summers worth of job experience, I worked an internship at ServiceNow in 2012 and at Sony Entertainment Network in 2013. I enjoy challenging work and how it increases my knowledge and programming skills. Tackling difficult problems is another hobby of mine, my Deep White application was built to allow myself to begin working on a Go AI (Go is one of the few games where computers can't beat humans).

If you are a potential employer and like what you see (or play), feel free to contact me any time.
